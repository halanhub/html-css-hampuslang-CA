Rainy Days Return Policy

Thank you for shopping with Rainy Days! We want you to be completely satisfied with your purchase. If you’re not happy with your order, we’re here to help.

Returns

  • You have 30 days from the date of purchase to return an item for a refund or exchange.
  • Items must be unused, unwashed, and in their original condition, with all tags and labels attached.
  • Proof of purchase is required for all returns.

Non-Returnable Items

  • final sale items
  • Gift cards
  • items purchased through third-party retailers

Refunds

  • Once we receive and inspect your return, we’ll notify you about the status of your refund.
  • Approved refunds will be issued to your original payment method within 7-10 business days.

Exchanges

  • We only replace items if they are defective or damaged. If you need to exchange an item for the same product, please contact us.

Shipping Returns

  • Customers are responsible for return shipping costs unless the item is defective or incorrect.
  • We recommend using a trackable shipping service or purchasing shipping insurance.

How to Start a Return

1. Contact us at return@rainydays.com, or through our Help page.

2. Include your order number, item details, and reason for the return.

3. We'll provide you with return instructions and the return adress.

If you have any questions or concerns, feel free to reach out — we’re here to help you push the comfort zone, no matter the weather!
